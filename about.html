This is TechServe.

In today's digital age, access to technology is more vital than ever before. However, many underprivileged communities still lack the necessary resources to bridge the digital divide. This is where Triangle TechServe Foundation comes in, with our admirable mission to collect, reset, and donate old computers to those in need. One of the key initiatives of Triangle TechServe Foundation is our commitment to holding fundraising events for various charitable causes. Through these events, we are able to raise awareness and support for important issues, while also providing leadership opportunities for those within the organization. Volunteers at Triangle TechServe Foundation can also benefit from the opportunity to verify our hours for clubs like Key Club and NHS, adding additional value to our philanthropic efforts.
