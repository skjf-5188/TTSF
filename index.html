Triangle TechServe Foundation

What is TechServe?

Triangle Techserve Foundation is a non-profit organization in the Triangle Area: Raleigh, Cary, and Durham.

What do we do?

We are committed to reducing the digital divide by ensuring equal access to technology, educating our communities, and building a connected future.
